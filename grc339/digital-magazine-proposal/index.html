Introduction

The Good Tourist

The Good Tourist (TGT) is a magazine that focuses on the best ways to educate yourself before traveling to a new place. It's important to research where you are going, how to contribute to their economy, how to behave appropriately, what type of tourism you should avoid, and so much more. TGT will link readers to useful resources as well as other "know before you go" tips and tricks.
